OrchardRoots ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information that you share with us when you use our website or order via WhatsApp. We operate as a Singapore-based business and take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Name — provided when placing an order or filling in a contact form.
  • Phone number / WhatsApp number — used to communicate your order and provide customer support.
  • Email address — if provided for order confirmation or enquiries.
  • Delivery address — if you request delivery within Singapore.
  • Order details — products, quantities, and order preferences shared via WhatsApp.

We do not collect payment card information directly. Payments are handled separately and securely outside this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfil your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your order via WhatsApp or email.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you product updates or promotions, only if you have opted in.
  • To improve our products and services based on feedback.

We will never use your information for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

3. WhatsApp Order Communication

OrchardRoots uses WhatsApp as a primary channel for receiving and managing orders. When you click "Checkout via WhatsApp" or "Order on WhatsApp", your browser opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing your order details. This message is sent directly from your WhatsApp account to our business number: +65 9692 6272.

By initiating a WhatsApp conversation with us, you consent to us viewing and storing your name, phone number, and order details shared in that conversation. WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs how WhatsApp handles your data. We encourage you to review it at whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil your order and meet our legal obligations. Order-related communication on WhatsApp is stored within the WhatsApp platform. If you wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us directly at orchardroots@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.

OrchardRoots does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively to serve you as a customer.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., WhatsApp, Google Maps). These websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to save your shopping cart between sessions. No tracking cookies are used. We do not use analytics tools or advertising trackers on this site. The information stored locally remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. All WhatsApp communications are protected by end-to-end encryption as provided by WhatsApp. Our website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure data transmission.

  • Request correction of inaccurate data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at orchardroots@gmail.com.
